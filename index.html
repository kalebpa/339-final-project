Our Club

Mission

Through its three pillars of Family, Excellence, and Joy, UMMCS seeks to develop and foster strong character within its members. With this structure in place, UMMCS will strive to provide an inclusive environment for members to share their love of soccer.

Pillars

Family

In victory and defeat

The best teams are the ones who play for one another. Establishing a sense of brotherhood amongst players creates an environment conducive to success.​

Excellence

On and off the field

This program upholds the highest of standards for its members. Effort, dedication, execution, and accountability are all pivotal to a well-oiled soccer machine.

Joy

To last a lifetime

​​We play the game for its most beautiful, moments. A ball fired into the top corner, the pandemonium of a last-minute triumph, and the immortality of our legacy.
